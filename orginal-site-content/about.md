# About Us - Source of Truth

## Dinks Pickleball - Now Open!

We are a local, family owned, dedicated indoor pickleball venue. In fact, we are the largest dedicated pickleball venue indoors or outdoors in central Iowa!

### Facility Details

- **Size:** Over 70,000 sq feet
- **Courts:** 13 dedicated courts
- **Amenities:**
  - Dedicated practice/warm up areas
  - Plenty of room to socialize with friends before and after play

### What We Offer

- Social play
- Competitive play
- Open play
- Leagues
- Tournaments
- Fun events

**New to pickleball?** No problem! We have professional instructors that will get you up to speed during our Intro to Pickleball clinics or help take your game to the next level!

**Impact of Pickleball:**

Pickleball has had a huge, positive impact on their lives:

- Made many life long friends
- Traveled to many states for tournaments
- Provides an outlet for competitiveness

**Why Dinks:**

Dinks represents both a passion for pickleball while at the same time an opportunity to respond to the overwhelming need for proper indoor facilities for the growing pickleball demographic in the greater Des Moines area.
