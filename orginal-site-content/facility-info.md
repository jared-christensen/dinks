# Facility Information - Source of Truth

## Facility Name

Dinks Pickleball

## Location

**Address:** 3800 Merle Hay Rd Suite 1000, Des Moines, IA 50310

**Details:**

- Located on the north end of Merle Hay Mall
- Next to the food court and Kids Empire

## Hours

**Open Daily:** 6 am – midnight

## Facility Details

### Courts

- **Total Courts:** 13 indoor courts
- **Climate Controlled:** Yes
- **Surface:** Resurfaced courts
- **Year-round Play:** Yes, no weather worries

### Description

- Iowa's largest dedicated indoor pickleball venue
- Built by local families for the Des Moines community
- Family-owned community hub

### What We Offer

- **Access:** Personal door code for members (6 am – midnight)
- **Booking:** Reserve courts via CourtReserve
- **Ball Machine:** $10 rental for members
- **Programming:**
  - Intro clinics
  - Ladders
  - Tournaments
  - Social play
  - Leagues
  - Member programming with early access perks

## Key Features

1. **Year-round courts** - Play from 6am–midnight on climate-controlled, resurfaced courts—no weather worries
2. **Programming for everyone** - Intro clinics, ladders, tournaments, and social play keep every skill level engaged
3. **Family-owned community hub** - Built by local families who wanted a true home for Des Moines pickleball
