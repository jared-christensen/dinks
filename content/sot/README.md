# Source of Truth - Dinks Pickleball

This directory contains the original content from the Dinks Pickleball website as a reference to ensure accuracy when making updates.

## Purpose

- Preserve original website content
- Ensure we don't make up information
- Reference for copy, facts, and details
- Historical record of site content

## Contents

- `home.md` - Home page content, taglines, court rates, and key messaging
- `about.md` - About Dinks and the founding families (Lanning & Douglass)
- `memberships.md` - Original membership tiers, pricing, and benefits
- `sponsors.md` - Current sponsors and their information
- `facility-info.md` - Facility details, hours, location
- `contact-info.md` - Contact information and communication channels
- `faq.md` - Frequently asked questions and answers
- `instruction.md` - Professional instruction, clinics, and destination trips
- `leagues-tournaments-events.md` - Leagues, tournaments, and events schedule

## Usage

Always check these files when:

- Writing new copy
- Updating home page content, taglines, or court rates
- Updating About page or family information
- Updating membership information
- Adding or modifying sponsor details
- Changing facility information
- Updating contact details
- Modifying FAQ content
- Updating instruction or clinic offerings
- Adding leagues, tournaments, or events information
